About Hintouch


Hintouch is the social platform revolutionizing creator and fan connections. The site is inclusive of artists and content creators from all genres and allows them to monetize their content while developing authentic relationships with their fanbase


Unlike other social platforms, Hintouch is welcoming of all creator genres and their content. The platform is designed to optimize creator engagement so when a creator posts, more of their fans see and interact with the content; and direct messages sent by creators are seen, opened, read and responded to. The platform will continue to grow as more creators are using content as a way to connect with their fans.



Our mission is to connect people meaningfully.


We work every day to be worthy of our users’ trust as we build a community and a business which reflects truth, transparency and meaningful connections. We welcome creativity in meeting our users’ needs and developing sustainable solutions.



We have created a platform that:

  • Helps creators make/post videos for their content and allow them charge for this or make it free to view to fans and users
  • Partners with movie producers to show Cinema only movies in the app for a charge
  • Allows Paid Live Streaming of shows and events in the app (if users need to vote in shows, voting will be allowed in-app too)
  • With the app you can subscribe to a creators page you love (with or without a membership fee), contact them, tip them and get direct connection with them.
  • You can also enjoy our monthly hintcouch-producer based movies, Cinema movies, and other movies created by creators using the app.
  • You can stream Live events from anywhere in the world, like comedy event, standup shows and parties. You can also watch live or already created shows.
  • As added benefit you can create paid classes, school, trainings, gym and a host of others that can be done using videos
